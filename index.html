Instructions

Use the Ctrl+Shift+C combination to activate the browser's in-built system of highlighthing and selecting. Once you select an element on the page by single clicking, the element will be highlighted in the 'Elements' section of the tools provided. This will help you to choose the best term to add to the list. Try to find the biggest containing element with the most specific 'class' or 'id' to choose as a unique term to remove. Please note: adding generic terms will often remove large sections or whole pages from view. When in doubt have your exported back-up ready and wipe the memory fresh if needed. Good Luck.
